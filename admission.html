About the Lab

Welcome to the Zhang_lab! Our lab is part of the School of Chemistry and Biological Engineering at the University of Science and Technology Beijing, with a primary research focus on computational molecular biology. Our key research areas include the application of molecular simulations to metal ions and biomacromolecular systems (particularly molecular dynamics simulations and molecular docking), computer-aided drug design, and bioinformatics-related research. We warmly welcome undergraduate and graduate students interested in drug design, computational biology, and molecular simulation to join Zhang Lab.

Admission Requirements

We invite outstanding students dedicated to pursuing research in computational fields to join our lab. The admission requirements are as follows:

  • Relevant research experience or interest in our research areas
  • Proficiency in English, with the ability to read and write scientific papers
  • Strong team spirit and communication skills.

Contact us at: zhanghy@ustb.edu.cn.
We look forward to your joining us!
